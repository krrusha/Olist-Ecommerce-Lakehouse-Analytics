Olist Ecommerce Lakehouse Analytics

Databricks SQL lakehouse project analyzing revenue concentration, fulfillment delays, and review impact with an interactive 2-page dashboard.

Note: The interactive Databricks dashboard requires workspace access. For reproducibility, see sql/ scripts and the exported artifacts in assets/ and databricks_exports/.

Dashboard

Insights

  • Revenue is geographically concentrated. São Paulo (SP) contributes ~37% of revenue in the selected range.
  • Late delivery is relatively low overall, but impactful. Roughly ~9% of items are delivered late; most late deliveries fall within 1–7 days.
  • Delivery delay strongly impacts customer satisfaction. Review scores drop significantly as delivery delays increase.
  • Late-delivery risk is not evenly distributed across seller states. A small number of seller states show higher late delivery rates.
  • Specific seller→customer routes are operational hotspots. Certain routes combine higher late-delivery rates with meaningful volume, increasing Estimated Late Orders.

What it does

  • Load: Raw Olist tables into a source schema (workspace.olist_lakehouse).
  • Curate: Build analytics-ready objects in workspace.olist_curated (including analytics_orders_with_date with a clean purchase_date).
  • Model in SQL: Create KPI + operational datasets designed for dashboard use.
  • Visualize: Publish a 2-page Databricks Dashboard with a global p_date date-range parameter.
